Computer Society of India

CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years. Today, the CSI has 72 chapters all over India, 511 student branches, and more than 100000 members including India's most famous IT industry leaders, brilliant scientists and dedicated academicians.

A brief history

  • First and Largest computer society in India.
  • Was started in 1965.
Computer Society of India is the first and the largest body of the computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals.

Why Join CSI

  • Partner of IEEE Computer Society.
  • Training and Certification in various research fields.
  • Funding in minor projects.
  • Publish papers under their banner.
  • CSI Minor research project funding up to Rs. 50000/-
